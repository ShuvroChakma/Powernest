Our Story

Two generations. One name.

In 1983, a single passion of delivering the best electronics drove our family to open our first shop in Kuala Lumpur. Today, that same family has grown to serve over a million customers and a hundred premium brands — united by the same belief: everyone deserves the finest quality.

Since 1983

43 years and counting

1983
The Beginning

The first store opened in Kuala Lumpur — a small family shop with a big dream to serve the nation with premium electronics.

2000
Southeast Asia's Biggest Superstore

Expanded to 8 branches across Malaysia — becoming the region's undisputed leader in consumer electronics.

2012
Multi-City Expansion

Opened showrooms in Penang, Johor Bahru, and Kuching — bringing our standards of excellence to every corner of Malaysia.

2018
eCommerce Launch

Launched our digital storefront, making our products accessible to international buyers across 30+ countries.

2022
North, Rangel & Khulna

Expanded internationally — partnering with distributors across South Asia and the Middle East for bulk supply agreements.

Today
Over a Million Customers Served

Today PowerNest is Malaysia's most trusted wholesale electronics partner — shipping globally, backed by ISO certification and 40+ years of excellence.
